About me!

Who is me?

My name is Panagiotis Kargos, I come from Alexandroupoli and I am a final year student at the public IEK of Alexandroupoli with a specialization in IT Application Technician. My main occupation is in the field of multimedia, but mostly the construction - composition of music. I use the program Audacity and FL Studio for my needs and I am thinking of moving professionally in this area in the long term. I hold a multimedia certification in video, audio, image editing as well as harmonioum lessons. Also, I have participated in various social music events during the harmonium lessons. I like, specifically, Jazz music, classical, byzantine, some techno, Hip hop, national music, and traditional. I am also actively involved in the art of chanting.
